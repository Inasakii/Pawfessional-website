Our Story

Founded with a passion for animals and a commitment to pet wellness, Pawfessional Vet Clinic began as a small neighborhood clinic in Rodriguez, Rizal. Over the years, we’ve grown into a trusted partner for pet owners, blending expert care with genuine compassion.

2022: Humble Beginnings

We opened our doors to provide accessible veterinary care to the local community.

2024: Growing Family

Expanded services to include grooming and preventive care, welcoming more pet families.

2025: Continuing Excellence

Launched new loyalty rewards and digital booking to make pet care easier than ever.
